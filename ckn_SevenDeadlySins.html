Synopsis : The Seven Deadly Sins, a group of legendary knights, disbanded after being accused of plotting to overthrow the kingdom. Years later, the Holy Knights staged a coup d'état and captured the king, becoming tyrannical rulers. The third princess, Elizabeth, sets out on a journey to find the Seven Deadly Sins and enlist their help to save the kingdom.
